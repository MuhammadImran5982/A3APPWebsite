Group Processes and Reflection

Group Processes and Communication

The group got off to a rocky start, having only Louisa doing the work and again a lack of communication towards the project until the other members realised that the deadline was coming to a close. Throughout the rest of the project group RDC has done a great job with communication remotely. There were two meetings a week and the majority of us showed up to contribute towards the meeting. Nonverbally wise the group was exceptional when it came to how to contact another for help and information requiring what needed to be done and projected information. Discord was our main direct contact manager whilst teams was our formal group discussion page where video meetings were also held. Someone members did struggle to help out and fill in gaps where it was needed but overall the group worked quite seamlessly together.
